🛡 CyberShield Lite – Compliance & Ethics

CyberShield Lite is committed to maintaining the highest standards of cybersecurity practice, privacy protection, and legal compliance. Our processes follow internationally recognized frameworks and Kenyan law.

🔒 Legal Authorization

We only perform scans on assets that our clients explicitly own or are authorized to test. All clients must accept our Terms of Service and submit consent via the request form before a scan is initiated.

📝 Consent & NDA

Every scan request includes:

  • ✅ A signed Scan Authorization Consent Form
  • 📎 A Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

These are automatically emailed to the client and archived securely for compliance and legal assurance.

🔐 Data Privacy

Client data (IP addresses, scan results, personal details) is never sold, shared, or analyzed beyond what's required to generate a report. See our Privacy Policy for details.

📅 Data Retention

Scan reports are retained for 30 days by default. Clients may request permanent deletion at any time via privacy@cybershieldlite.com.

⚖ Compliance Framework

CyberShield Lite aligns with:

  • 🛡 Kenya Data Protection Act (2019)
  • 🌐 Basic GDPR principles (transparency, data minimization)
  • 📄 Industry-standard CVSS & CVE reporting practices

🚫 No Unauthorized Use

CyberShield Lite will never scan government, corporate, or public systems without explicit authorization. Our services are not for penetration testing or exploit delivery.

What is CyberShield Lite?

CyberShield Lite is an affordable, on-demand cybersecurity scanning service built for small businesses, NGOs, and individuals. Powered by Nmap and OpenVAS, it detects vulnerabilities, highlights critical exposures, and provides a PDF report with CVEs, CVSS scores, and patch recommendations.
